DIRECTIONS

ALT-Boston 2017 will take place at the Boston Esplanade. Stage 1 is located at Lederman Park, while Stage 2 is located at the DCR Hatch Memorial Shell. Parking is not available, and all attendees are encouraged to take public transportation or ride-sharing services. Event staff and security will be present to help usher guests between stages.
